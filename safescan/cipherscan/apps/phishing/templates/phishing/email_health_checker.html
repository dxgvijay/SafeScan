ℹ What This Means

📧

MX Records (Mail Exchange)

Mail exchange records tell other servers where to deliver email for this domain. Without MX records, the domain cannot receive any email.

🛡️

SPF Record (Sender Policy Framework)

Sender Policy Framework prevents domain spoofing by specifying which servers are authorized to send email for this domain. Missing or misconfigured SPF makes the domain vulnerable to spoofing.

🔐

DMARC Policy (Domain-Based Message Authentication)

Domain-based Message Authentication prevents phishing by telling receiving servers what to do with emails that fail SPF or DKIM checks. A DMARC policy is essential for domain protection.
